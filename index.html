My Journey

  • 2013-2019

    A Humble Begining

    My first experience with computer engineerging came as a founder of a startup company that tried to leverage the use of mobile and web design to help physical therapist connect with their clients. The mobile app would allow PT's give individualized programs that would utilize photo and video instruction. I was responsible for designing and implementing front end and backend frameworks, flow patterns and resolving all queries from our development team.

  • June 2021

    General Assembly

    After 10 years in the healthcare industry, I made the decision to continue to expand myself and step outside of my comfort zone. I enrolled into General Assembly's Full Stack Computer Engineering Bootcamp which I knew would provide me with the greatest foundation for a sucessful career.

  • October 2021

Each time I solve a problem it increases my confidence and allows me to continually push the boundaries of my pre-concieved limits.
